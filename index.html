What is this?

MmmDot is an innovative game that merges creativity and problem solving as users complete puzzles by connecting dots to create music. It is an interactive sound platform, where plays will need to replicate musical tracks with visual cues/patterns.

How do I play?

Use the keyboard numbers 1, 2, or 3 to switch between instruments.

To draw the line, click on one point and drag to another point.

To delete the line, click on one point of the line, and press 'tab'.

To play what you have, press the 'play what i have' button.

Press the 'play what i am supposed to play' button to hear what you're supposed to play
